## **1. Introduction**

Chaindesk, registered at 65 rue de l’Ourcq, Paris (France) takes your privacy seriously and considers it important for your personal data (hereafter “your data”) to be treated with the necessary care and confidentiality at all times.

This Privacy Statement explains how our organization uses the personal data we collect from you when you and why. If you have any questions after reading this Privacy Statement, feel free to contact us at [support@chaindesk.ai](mailto:support@chaindesk.ai).

To avoid any misunderstandings, we would like to clarify that this Privacy Statement is applicable on the processing of personal data from:

- Creators: the person who creates and distributes Chaindesk
- Website visitors: persons who visit our marketing landing page

Chaindesk is a provider of this service and not the owner of the collected data. The creator (the person who created the chatbot) is responsible for the data he/she collects and thus data controller of respondent data.

## **2. What data do we collect**

Information we collect from **Creators** (you hold a Chaindesk account and you are using our service) or **Website visitors** (you are visiting our website).

- **Electronical identification data**: including IP address, device & browser data, and location data;
- **Information about your use of our website**: including how you ended up on our website and the pages you visited.
- **Registration information**
  When you register for an account, we collect your first and last name, username, password, and email address.
- **Billing information**
  If you pay for a Chaindesk service, we will ask for your billing details, name, address, and financial information depending on your payment method (stored by our payment service provider Stripe)
- **Chatbot** **data**
  We store your chatbot data for you and provide tools for you to use with respect to this data.

## **3. How we use your data**

Chaindesk does not sell personal data to third parties or use it for marketing purposes or for serving advertisements. We have no interest in collecting non-anonymized data from anyone who isn’t a registered user of Chaindesk.

We only collect your data with your consent or in order to:

- Deliver our service to you
- To improve your user experience
- To email you with essential product updates
- To troubleshoot product functionality and fix bugs
- To respond to legal requests or prevent fraud

## **4. Data we share**

We only share your information with our service providers who help us operate our business, in which case those third parties are required to comply with the GDPR framework.

You can find a list of sub-processors on our [GDPR Page](/privacy/gdpr).

## **5. Data retention**

If you are a Chaindesk chatbot creator we do not delete the data in your account. You are responsible for the time period for which you store the data. You can delete data in your Chaindesk account. If you are a respondent, you will need to ask the creator how long your responses will be stored in Chaindesk.

We honor all deletions, and all chatbot data that has been deleted by the chatbot creator is permanently deleted from our back-ups within 90 days.
